# SecureHorizon Marketing Campaign Strategy

## Brand Positioning
**Tagline:** "Protect What Matters Most"
**Voice:** Warm, trustworthy, empathetic — never salesy. Speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a pushy agent.

---

## Target Audiences

### 1. Young Families (Ages 28-42)
- **Pain:** "What happens to my kids if something happens to me?"
- **Trigger:** New baby, buying a home, getting married
- **Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, Google Search

### 2. Pre-Retirees (Ages 50-65)
- **Pain:** "Will I outlive my savings?"
- **Trigger:** Approaching retirement, market volatility anxiety
- **Channels:** Facebook, Google Search, YouTube, Email

### 3. Small Business Owners (Ages 30-55)
- **Pain:** "If I can't work, my family and employees are in trouble"
- **Trigger:** Growing business, hiring employees, key-person dependency
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, Google Search, Facebook

---

## Campaign 1: Facebook & Instagram Ads

### Ad Set A — "The What-If" (Young Families)
**Hook:** "If something happened to you tomorrow, could your family keep their home?"
**Body:** Most families are underinsured by $200,000. In just 2 minutes, find out what coverage you actually need — for free. No obligation. No sales calls unless you want them.
**CTA:** "Get My Free Coverage Analysis →"
**Creative:** Warm photo of parent with children, soft lighting, text overlay with stat

### Ad Set B — "Never Run Out" (Pre-Retirees)
**Hook:** "What if your retirement paycheck never stopped — no matter how long you live?"
**Body:** Annuities can provide guaranteed income for life. See how much you could receive — free personalized estimate in 2 minutes.
**CTA:** "Calculate My Retirement Income →"
**Creative:** Happy retired couple outdoors, golden hour, overlaid with "$3,200/mo guaranteed for life" text

### Ad Set C — "Built to Last" (Business Owners)
**Hook:** "You built your business from scratch. But who protects it if you can't work?"
**Body:** Key-person insurance and business protection plans designed by advisors who understand entrepreneurs. Get a custom plan — free.
**CTA:** "Protect My Business →"
**Creative:** Professional at desk, confident pose, clean design

### Targeting Settings
- Location: United States
- Age: Per audience segment
- Interests: Personal finance, retirement planning, family, homeownership, small business
- Lookalike audiences from existing leads
- Retarget website visitors who didn't complete the form

## Campaign 3: Email Nurture Sequence (Post-Lead Capture)

### Email 1 — Immediate (Auto-send)
**Subject:** "Your personalized quote is on the way, {firstName}"
**Body:** Thank them, set expectations (24hr advisor contact), link to a quick "Insurance 101" guide, include advisor's photo/name for trust.

### Email 2 — Day 2
**Subject:** "The #1 mistake families make with insurance"
**Body:** Educational content about being underinsured. Stats about coverage gaps. Soft CTA to schedule a call.

### Email 3 — Day 5
**Subject:** "{firstName}, here's what $50/month could protect"
**Body:** Show tangible examples of what different coverage levels provide. Include client success story. CTA to talk to an advisor.

### Email 4 — Day 10
**Subject:** "A quick note from your advisor"
**Body:** Personal, short email from the advisor. "Just checking in, I know life gets busy." Include direct calendar link to book a call.

### Email 5 — Day 21
**Subject:** "Time-sensitive: Rate changes coming"
**Body:** Urgency-based — rates increase with age, health changes can affect eligibility. "Lock in today's rates while you qualify." Strong CTA.

## Campaign 5: Social Media Organic Content

### Weekly Content Calendar
- **Monday:** Motivational quote about financial security + stat

- **Wednesday:** Client success story (with permission)
- **Thursday:** Quick tip / myth-buster

- **Instagram:** Stories with polls ("Do you have life insurance? Yes/No"), Reels with quick tips

## Conversion Optimization Tips

1. **A/B Test Headlines:** Rotate between fear-based ("Don't leave your family unprotected") and aspiration-based ("Give your family financial freedom")
2. **Social Proof:** Display lead count on the site ("Join 10,000+ families")
3. **Exit Intent Popup:** Offer a free "Coverage Gap Calculator" PDF when users try to leave
4. **Retargeting:** Show testimonial ads to users who visited but didn't convert
5. **Urgency Elements:** "Rates go up on your next birthday" messaging near the CTA

## KPIs to Track

- **Website visitors** (goal: 5,000+/month)
- **Form completion rate** (goal: 15-25% of visitors to /quote)
- **Cost per lead** (goal: $15-$40)
- **Lead-to-consultation rate** (goal: 40-60%)
- **Consultation-to-client rate** (goal: 20-35%)
- **Customer lifetime value** (track over time)
